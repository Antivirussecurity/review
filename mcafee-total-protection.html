What Is McAfee Total Protection?

The origins of McAfee trace back to 1987 when John McAfee founded the company to create antivirus software. Over the years, McAfee has evolved from focusing solely on antivirus protection to offering a broad range of security services, including identity theft protection and internet security.

McAfee Total Protection is its most recent packaged product, offering comprehensive protection against viruses, malware, and online threats. It includes antivirus protection, a firewall, web and email protection, a file shredder for securely deleting sensitive data and a Password Manager for secure password storage and management.
