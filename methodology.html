Our Review Process

A clear, step-by-step flow for how we test, score and rank every antivirus and security suite.

01
Research & Selection
  • Identify leading and emerging antivirus products from trusted vendors.
  • Consider popularity, regional availability, user feedback and lab recognition.
  • Shortlist plans that are relevant for home, business, and multi-device users.
02
Testing Environment Setup
  • Use clean virtual machines and physical devices with different OS and specs.
  • Simulate real user conditions ‚Äî browsing, downloads, office work, gaming.
  • Prepare controlled malware, phishing and unsafe website test kits.
03
Feature & Protection Analysis
  • Check real-time scanning, on-demand scans and removable drive protection.
  • Test ransomware shields, web protection, firewall, anti-phishing and email filters.
  • Evaluate VPN quality, password manager, parental controls and identity tools (where available).
04
Performance & Usability Assessment
  • Measure CPU, RAM and disk impact during idle, quick scan and full scan.
  • Check boot time delay, gaming mode effectiveness and background scheduling.
  • Review design, navigation, settings clarity and ease for non-technical users.
05
Support, Pricing & Value
  • Test support channels (chat, email, phone, knowledge base) for responsiveness.
  • Compare pricing tiers, device limits, renewal prices and bundled extras.
  • Calculate overall value-for-money score vs features and protection quality.
06
Final Scoring & Ranking
  • Apply weightage across our core scoring parameters (see below).
  • Benchmark products against each other within their category & use-case.
  • Update rankings when vendors release major updates or security fixes.
